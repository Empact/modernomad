Embassy Network

The Embassy Network is a collection of coliving houses prototyping a new approach to Home, one in which members flow freely between locations as they travel the globe for work and collaboration.

Embassy locations are united through the values of co-creation, curiosity and intention. These embassies and their residents are ambassadors of a modern culture of openness, collaboration, and connectivity.
